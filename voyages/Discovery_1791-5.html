HMS Discovery (1791-5)¶

HMS Discovery was commanded by George Vancouver on a voyage to chart the American Pacific Northwest.

Weather observations were made by the expedition naturalist and surgeon Archibalkd Menzies, and recorded in his journal, which is preserved in the library of the Royal Meteorological Society.
