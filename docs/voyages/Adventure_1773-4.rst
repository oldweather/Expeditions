HMS Adventure (1773-4)
======================

`HMS Adventure <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/HMS_Adventure_(1771)>`_ was on `James Cook's <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/James_Cook>`_ `Second expedition <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Second_voyage_of_James_Cook>`_. (Along with :doc:`HMS Resolution <Resolution_1772-5>`).

Their observations were published in `A voyage towards the South pole, and round the world. <https://open.library.ubc.ca/collections/chung/chungpub/items/1.0129126>`_
