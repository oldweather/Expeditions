Princesse Alice II Spitzbergen expedition (1898)
================================================

The Princesse Alice II, was a yacht owned by  `Albert I, Prince of Monaco <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Albert_I,_Prince_of_Monaco>`_. In 1898 it undertook a research voyage to Spitzbergen.

The log of the voyage has been preserved by the `Ocenographic Museum of Monaco <https://www.visitmonaco.com/us/place/monuments-museums/76/oceanographic-museum-of-monaco>`_. Weather observations in the log are limited, but it does contain a sequence of barometer observations.
