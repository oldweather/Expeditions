SMS Planet voyage of 1906-7
===========================

.. warning::

   This expedition is not yet released.

`SMS Planet <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/SMS_Planet_(1905)>`_ was a German Navy survey ship comissioned in 1905.

In 1906-7 the ship was on a survey voyage from Germany to Hong Kong, and the weather observations makde on the voyage have been transcribed from the printed reports.
