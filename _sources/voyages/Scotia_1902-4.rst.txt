Scotia (1902-4)
===============

Scotia was the ship of the `Scottish National Antarctic Expedition <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Scottish_National_Antarctic_Expedition>`_ led by `William Speirs Bruce <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/William_Speirs_Bruce>`_.

Their observations were published in `Report of the Scientific Results of the Voyage of S.Y. "Scotia" <https://www.biodiversitylibrary.org/bibliography/31764#/summary>`_.

As well as the ship observations, included here. The expedition established the permanant weather station at `Orcadas <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Orcadas_Base>`_. Orcadas observations are *not* included in this dataset.
