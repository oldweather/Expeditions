HMS Beagle (1831-6)
===================

`HMS Beagle <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/HMS_Beagle>`_, commanded by `Robert Fitzroy <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Robert_FitzRoy>`_ set out in 1831 on `a survey expedition to South America <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Second_voyage_of_HMS_Beagle>`_.

Their observations were published in `Narrative of the surveying voyages of His Majesty's Ships Adventure and Beagle ... <https://www.biodiversitylibrary.org/bibliography/49451#/summary>`_.
