HMS Resolution (1772-5)
=======================

`HMS Resolution <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/HMS_Resolution_(1771)>`_ was on `James Cook's <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/James_Cook>`_ `Second expedition <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Second_voyage_of_James_Cook>`_. (Along with :doc:`HMS Adventure <Adventure_1773-4>`).

Their observations were published in `A voyage towards the South pole, and round the world. <https://open.library.ubc.ca/collections/chung/chungpub/items/1.0129126>`_

On board the Resolution was astronomer `William Wales <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/William_Wales_(astronomer)>`_ who kept `a log of the voyage <https://cudl.lib.cam.ac.uk/view/MS-RGO-00014-00058/1>`_ containing additional weather observations.
