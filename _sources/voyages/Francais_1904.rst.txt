Francais (1904)
===============

The Francais was the ship of the `Third French Antarctic Expedition <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/French_Antarctic_Expedition>`_, commanded by `Jean-Baptiste Charcot <https://www.coolantarctica.com/Antarctica%20fact%20file/History/antarctic_whos_who_charcot_francais.php>`_

Their observations were published in `Towards the South Pole Aboard the Fran√ßais: The First French Expedition to the Antarctic, 1903-1905 <https://books.google.co.uk/books?id=p8wPAQAAIAAJ>`_.
