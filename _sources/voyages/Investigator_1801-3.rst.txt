HMS Investigator (1801-3)
=========================

`HMS Investigator <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/HMS_Investigator_(1801)>`_ made a survey voyage to map the Australian coastline, under the command of `Matthew Flinders <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Matthew_Flinders>`_.

Their observations were published in `Flinders' personal journal <https://cudl.lib.cam.ac.uk/view/MS-RGO-00014-00064/3>`_.
