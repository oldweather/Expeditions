Porquoi Pas (1908-10)
=====================

`Porquois Pas <https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Pourquoi-Pas_(1908)&_IV=>`_ was the ship of `Jean-Baptiste Charcot <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jean-Baptiste_Charcot>`_ 's second Antarctic expedition.

Their observations were published in `Le pourquoi-pas dans l'Antarctique <https://www.biodiversitylibrary.org/bibliography/100033#/summary>`_.
