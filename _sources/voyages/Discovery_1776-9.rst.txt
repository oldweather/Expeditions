HMS Discovery (1776-9)
======================

`HMS Discovery <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/HMS_Discovery_(1774)>`_ was on `James Cook's <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/James_Cook>`_  `Third expedition <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Third_voyage_of_James_Cook>`_  (Along with :doc:`HMS Resolution <Resolution_1779-80>`).

Their observations were published in `A Voyage to the Pacific Ocean ... <https://books.google.co.uk/books?id=Mtfuhns48XQC>`_
