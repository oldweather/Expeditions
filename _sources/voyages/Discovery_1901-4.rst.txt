RRS Discovery (1901-4)
======================

`RRS Discovery <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/RRS_Discovery>`_ was the ship, and main base, of the `British National Antarctic Expedition <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Discovery_Expedition>`_, commanded by `Robert Falcon Scott <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Robert_Falcon_Scott>`_.

The expedition reports included exerpts from the Meteorological log of the Discovery (though only for the period when she was frozen into Winter Quarters Bay). These are the observations included here.
