Princess Louise (1849)
======================

The `Princess Louise <http://www.slsa.sa.gov.au/BSA/1849PrincessLouise.htm>`_ carried immigrants from Hamburg to Adelaide.

One of the passengers, Otto Schomburgk, kept a meteorological diary of the voyage.
