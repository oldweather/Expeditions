Naturaliste (1800-3)
====================

`Nicolas Baudin <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nicolas_Baudin>`_ commanded a French expedition to survey the coast of Australia. Baudin's ship was the `Geographe <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/French_corvette_G%C3%A9ographe>`_, and he was supported by the `Naturaliste <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/French_corvette_Naturaliste>`_ under `Jacques Hamelin <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jacques_F%C3%A9lix_Emmanuel_Hamelin>`_.
