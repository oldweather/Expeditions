Terra Nova (1910-13)
====================

`Terra Nova <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Terra_Nova_(ship)>`_ was the ship of the `British Antarctic Expedition 1910-13 <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Terra_Nova_Expedition>`_ led by `Robert Falcon Scott <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Robert_Falcon_Scott>`_.

Their observations were published, by `George Simpson <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/George_Simpson_(meteorologist)>`_, in the expedition reports. These include abstracts from the Terra Nova's meteorological log for the three voyages from New Zealand to Antarctica.
