Boussole (1785-88)
==================

`La Pérouse <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jean-Fran%C3%A7ois_de_Galaup,_comte_de_Lap%C3%A9rouse>`_ commanded the Boussole (along with the :doc:`Astrolabe <Astrolabe_1785-87>`) on a French-government-sponsored scientific circumnavigation voyage. 

Their observations were published in `Voyage de La Pérouse autour du monde <https://www.biodiversitylibrary.org/bibliography/15861#/summary>`_
