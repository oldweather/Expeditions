Imperial Trans-Antarctic Expedition (1914-17)
=============================================

The `Imperial Trans-Antarctic Expedition <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Imperial_Trans-Antarctic_Expedition>`_, led by `Ernest Shackleton <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ernest_Shackleton>`_, had two main ships: `Aurora <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/SY_Aurora>`_ and `Endurance <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Endurance_(1912_ship)>`_; one major boat journey in the `James Caird <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Voyage_of_the_James_Caird>`_, and several rescue voyages, including one in the `Yelcho <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Yelcho_(1906)>`_.

Their observations were never published, but many of the original records of the expedition have been preserved in the archives of `The Scott Polar Research Institute <https://www.spri.cam.ac.uk/>`_.
