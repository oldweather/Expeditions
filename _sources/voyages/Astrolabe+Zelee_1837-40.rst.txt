Astrolabe and Zelee (1837-40)
=============================

`Dumont d'Urville <Dumont d'Urville>`_ commanded a French expedition to the Southern Ocean in the Ships `Astrolabe and Zelee <https://www.coolantarctica.com/Antarctica%20fact%20file/History/antarctic_ships/L%27Astrolabe_Zelee.php>`_.

Their observations were published in `Voyage au pole sud et dans l'Océanie sur les corvettes l'Astrolabe et la Zélée <https://www.biodiversitylibrary.org/bibliography/65972#/summary>`_
