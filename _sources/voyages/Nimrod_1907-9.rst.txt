Nimrod (1907-9)
===============

`Nimrod <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nimrod_(ship)>`_ was the expedition ship of the `British Antarctic Expedition <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nimrod_Expedition>`_ led by `Ernest Shackleton <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ernest_Shackleton>`_.

Exerpts from the ship's meteorological log (only the legs from New Zealand to Antarctica and back) were included in the expedition reports. These are the observations included here.
