Belgica (1897-99)
=================

`Belgica <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/RV_Belgica_(1884)>`_ was the ship of the `Belgian Antarctic Expedition <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Belgian_Antarctic_Expedition>`_ commanded by `Adrien de Gerlache <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Adrien_de_Gerlache>`_.

Their observations were published in "Rapport sur les observations météorologiques horaires" by Henryk Arctowski.
