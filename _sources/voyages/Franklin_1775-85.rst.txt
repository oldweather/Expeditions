Franklin (1775-85)
==================

`Benjamin Franklin <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Benjamin_Franklin>`_ made sea-temperature observations on at least two observations while crossing the Atlantic from France to the USA.

He published some observations in `a letter to David Leroy <http://oll.libertyfund.org/titles/2660#lf1438-11_head_058>`_, read at a meeting of the American Philosophical Society, December 2, 1785
