USS Vincennes (1838-42)
=======================

`USS Vincennes <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/USS_Vincennes_(1826)>`_ was the flagship of the `United States Exploring Expedition <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States_Exploring_Expedition>`_, commanded by `Charles Wilkes <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Charles_Wilkes>`_.

Their observations were published in `the expedition reports <https://www.biodiversitylibrary.org/item/75890#page/11/mode/1up>`_.
