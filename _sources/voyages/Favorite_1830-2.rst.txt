La Favorite (1830-32)
=====================

Favorite sailed around the world under the command of `Cyrille Pierre Théodore Laplace <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cyrille_Pierre_Th%C3%A9odore_Laplace>`_.

Their observations were published in `Voyage autour du monde par les mers de'lInde et de Chine exécuté sur la corvette de'létat la Favorite ... <https://www.biodiversitylibrary.org/bibliography/16403#/summary>`_.
