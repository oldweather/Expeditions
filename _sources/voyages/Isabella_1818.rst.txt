HMS Isabella (1818)
===================

HMS Isabella, commanded by `John Ross <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/John_Ross_(Royal_Navy_officer)>`_, accompanied by HMS Alexander (commanded by William Parry), set out to cross the NorthWest Passage via Baffin Bay. They got only a litle way into Lancaster Sound before finding their way blocked by the non-existent Croker Mountains.

The reported their observations in `A Voyage of Discovery: Made Under the Orders of the Admiralty, in His Majesty's Ships Isabella and Alexander, for the Purpose of Exploring Baffin's Bay, and Inquiring Into the Probability of a North-west Passage <https://books.google.co.uk/books/about/A_Voyage_of_Discovery.html?id=kPDormiHhzsC>`_
